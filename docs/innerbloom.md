# Halcyon Innerbloom Program

Welcome to the official documentation for the Halcyon Innerbloom program. This page provides a comprehensive overview of what the program is, why it exists, how it supports our development, and how you can participate.

---

## 1. What is Halcyon Innerbloom?

Halcyon Innerbloom is an exclusive, community-backed support program designed for users who wish to contribute financially to the sustainability of the Halcyon Project.

As a token of our deepest gratitude, donors who meet a specific contribution threshold are granted access to **custom, tailored builds** of Halcyon, allowing them to request specific, bespoke features that are not included in the standard public releases.

---

## 2. Background & Mission

Since its inception, Halcyon has been built on a **minimalist, stock-plus philosophy**. Our main goal has always been—and will always be—to provide a clean, fluid, and high-quality Android experience completely free of charge.

However, maintaining an independent custom ROM project comes with real-world hurdles:

- **A Team of Students:** The vast majority of our core team and device maintainers are high school and university students. We do not have corporate backing or steady engineering salaries; our personal budgets and allowances are naturally limited.

- **High-Performance Build Servers:** Compiling Android from source requires immense CPU, RAM, and storage resources. Running these server instances costs real money every month, which has historically been paid out of our own pockets.

- **Maintenance & Personal Time:** Integrating, cherry-picking, and troubleshooting custom feature requests per user requires hours of dedicated development time balanced alongside our academic responsibilities.

Previously, we offered custom builds for any donation amount. While we deeply appreciate every single cent, compiling separate custom builds for very small amounts has led to severe server resource exhaustion and unsustainable financial deficits for our student-led team. To keep the project alive, protect our team's well-being, and prevent our build servers from shutting down, we have structured the Innerbloom program to balance community appreciation with infrastructure reality.

---

## 3. Clearing the Misconception: "Are Features Paid?"

We want to address a common misunderstanding within the community: **Halcyon does NOT sell features**, and we do not put code behind a commercial paywall.

- **What you are supporting:** When you participate in Innerbloom, your donation goes directly toward renting the server compute power needed to compile code specifically for your request.

- **Supporter features are exclusive:** Custom features built for Innerbloom supporters are tailored specifically for them and will **not** be merged into the main source code. The only exception is if a requested feature is deemed broadly useful *and* aligns with our project's feature guidelines — in which case it may be considered for an official release, at the team's discretion.

- **The Framing:** The custom build is not a product you buy; it is a **bespoke token of appreciation** from us to you for keeping our servers running. Without your support, the server instance required to build your custom request literally could not exist.

---

## 4. Minimum Support Tier & Benefits

To ensure fairness and sustain our server costs, we have established a clear baseline for the Innerbloom custom build program:

### Innerbloom Supporter Tier

| Detail | Information |
|---|---|
| **Minimum Donation** | $8 USD / month (or equivalent in local currency) |
| **Core Benefit** | Access to Custom Tailored Builds with bespoke feature requests |
| **Build Frequency Limit** | Maximum of **2 custom build requests** per 30-day period |

> **Note:** Feature requests must be technically feasible and compatible with the source tree. Approved features are built exclusively for the requesting supporter and will not be included in public releases unless they meet our feature guidelines.

### Why the 2x Per Month Limit?

Compiling an Android ROM is an intensive process that takes time and server bandwidth. Limiting requests to twice a month ensures that:

- Every donor gets a **fair turn** in the build queue without facing massive delays.
- The server doesn't get overloaded by building daily for minor upstream commits.
- It encourages donors to **bundle their feature requests** efficiently.

---

## 5. Request Scope & Review Process

### What's Covered

Innerbloom custom build requests are scoped **exclusively to Halcyon's own source code**. This means we can accommodate things like custom system features, UI tweaks, behavior changes, or integrations that live within the Halcyon platform layer.

The following are **outside the scope** of Innerbloom and will not be fulfilled:

- **KernelSU (KSU)** or any other kernel-level root solutions
- **Custom recoveries** such as TWRP or any of its forks
- **Third-party ROM forks** or modifications derived from other projects
- Anything that falls outside of what the Halcyon source tree owns or maintains

If you are unsure whether your request falls within scope, feel free to ask **@HeroBuxx** on Telegram before submitting.

### Review & Acceptance

Every request submitted by a supporter goes through a review process by our team. We evaluate each request based on:

- **Technical feasibility** — whether it can be implemented within our source tree
- **Team capability** — whether we currently have the bandwidth and expertise to deliver it

If a request passes both criteria, we will proceed with the build. If it does not — whether due to technical limitations, incompatibility, or resource constraints — **we reserve the right to decline the request**. In such cases, we will inform you of the reason and, where possible, suggest alternatives.

## 6. How to Join

:::warning Device Eligibility
Innerbloom custom builds are only available for **officially supported Halcyon devices**. If your device is not on the official device list, you must **confirm eligibility with @HeroBuxx on Telegram first** before proceeding with any donation.
:::

If you want to support the project and get your own personalized version of Halcyon:

1. **Check that your device is officially supported.** If it is not, reach out to **@HeroBuxx** on Telegram to confirm eligibility before donating.
2. Head over to our [Donation Page](/donate) and choose your preferred payment method.
3. Ensure your monthly contribution meets or exceeds the **$8 USD** threshold.
4. Submit your donation proof to **@HeroBuxx** on Telegram.
5. Our build system or maintainer will process your request based on the queue.
